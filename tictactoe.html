TicTacToe

This game is for two players. One player is (x or X) which is represented by Pretty Pink and the other player is (o or O) represented by Gleeful Green. The goal is to get three boxes in a row (across, down, or diagonally). When you enter your letter into a box and click OK, the box will change to the listed color. The game ends with either one player winning or there is a tie.
